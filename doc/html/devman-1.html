1. About this Developers Manual

1.1 Purpose / Scope of this Developers Manual

This document explains the internals of the Etherboot package. The information here applies to version 5.0 of Etherboot.

1.2 Obtaining the most recent version of this document

This document and related documents are also kept online at the Etherboot Home Page. This will in general have the latest source distributions and documentation.
